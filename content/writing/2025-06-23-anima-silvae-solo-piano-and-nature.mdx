I have been making music for more than 30 years and a lot of that music has been design to work along pictures - so i’m but slow on the uptake here!  I kind knew it BUT now I feel it!

 Something magical happens when you combine sounds of nature, birds, animals, wind, rain - with Improvised music - Somehow our brains look for the connections, subtly shift our mode of perception where all the sounds dance and harmonise. Have a listen to this track - 'Anima Silvae' - improvised piano which I then added some field recordings from a museum sound commission I worked on years ago. - what do you think?
